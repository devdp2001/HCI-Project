Term Project for CSCI 4800 Fall 2022

Human-Computer Interaction






Synopsis:

The term project for this course consists of 4 milestones:


Milestone 1
identify a theme-relevant, computing-related task / problem


Milestone 2
evaluate the task / problem


Milestone 3
develop interface design alternatives for the task / problem


Milestone 4
implement a prototype of your design


This semester, the term project theme is “educational technology” (EdTech) in “computing education” at institutes of higher learning.

The eLearning Commons (eLC) is the online learning management system for the University of Georgia used everyday by students and professors.

Our team has chosen to focus on the issues regarding eLC's navigation process for assignments and the calendar.
